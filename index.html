Titan’s Covenant



The driver’s suit of Sky Titan – Premium Version, created by IDAI (Imagine the Directions of Artificial Intelligence), is designed as a living extension of the vehicle. Where Sky Titan embodies strength and guardianship, the suit translates that vision into human form.

Built from red and blue armor, it reflects dual spirit—red for valor, blue for serenity. Lightweight plating ensures protection while allowing fluid movement. Luminous veins across the chest and arms mirror the Titan’s energy channels, binding man and machine in covenant.

At its core lies a glowing emblem, pressed close to the heart. More than decoration, it seals vows of mastery, charity, faith, and holistic care. The emblem also bears IDAI’s mark—a radiant lion, symbolizing courage and guardianship.

Beyond symbolism, the suit carries advanced survival systems. In emergencies, it activates an ejection method, propelling the driver safely away. Thus, Titan’s Covenant is not only armor but a declaration of values, protection, and trust.
